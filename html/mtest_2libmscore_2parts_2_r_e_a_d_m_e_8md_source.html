2 =====================
3 
4 **part-_{1}_-_{2}_.mscx**
5 
6 _{1}_ which element is tested
7 
8 * `empty` -> just notes
9 * `all` -> all or as much as possible elements
10 * _element name_ -> tests for a specific element (fingering, symbol etc...)
11 
12 
13 {2} action - which action is tested for the given element
14 
15 * _empty string_ = no parts created
16 * `parts` == parts created
17 * _actions for elements_ == (add, uadd, uradd, del, udel, urdel) for elements
18 * _actions for all_ == ()
